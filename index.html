About Us

Logotech Clothing, a privately-owned company based in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, is dedicated to the sustainable procurement and production of textile goods. At Logotech Clothing, we prioritize building strong partnerships with our employees, customers, vendors, and all those associated with us or interested in joining our community. We extend a warm welcome to everyone.

While our journey began as a T-shirt manufacturing unit, our expertise now lies in producing Kids Wear, and we have also excelled in Women’s Resort wear through our in-house brand, CHANGE!, established in 2008. Through collaborations with esteemed global brands, we have garnered extensive knowledge and exposure, leading to significant growth over the past 21 years.

We are proud to have worked with designer labels, brands, retail companies, store chains, from across the globe with our clients based in Europe, U.K, U.S. Australia etc. The factory is spread over 15,000 square feet, employing over 150 skilled professionals. In addition, our raw material sourcing operations are spread across India, China etc.

We take pride in our certifications, including GOTS, GRS, and Fairtrade, which reflect our commitment to sustainable practices.
