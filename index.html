About Us

Logotech Clothing, based in Kolkata, India, is a sustainable textile company founded in 2002. Originally focused on T-shirt manufacturing, it has evolved to specialize in Kids Wear and Women's Resort wear through its brand CHANGE! established in 2008. With two decades of experience, Logotech has collaborated with global brands and worked with clients in Europe, U.K, U.S.

Certified with GOTS, GRS, and Fairtrade, Logotech practices sustainable procurement and production. The company provides end-to-end manufacturing services, ensuring quality checks for the production of over 25,000 pieces monthly. Committed to ecological responsibility, Logotech follows pollution control and energy conservation guidelines.

The factory maintains socially responsible practices, prohibiting child labor and adhering to SA 8000 and Fairtrade compliance. Actively promoting gender inclusivity, Logotech creates stable job opportunities for women and emphasizes the well-being of its workforce through health camps, yoga sessions, and festive celebrations. With a focus on employee partnerships and global collaborations, Logotech Clothing continues to contribute positively to the fashion industry.
